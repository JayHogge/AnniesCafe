Welcome to Annie's Cafe

Annie's

Super cute and family-owned, we're Bountiful's finest. Our breakfast and lunch menu is crepe-centric with savory favorites such as the ham, egg and cheese Sunrise and the loaded potato crepe topped with country sausage gravy RIC.



Our sweet crepes are very popular with our Peaches and Cream and our Nutella Banana crepe leading the pack of favorites. We also offer soups, salads, a large selection of sandwiches and gourmet hamburgers.
